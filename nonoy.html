SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all

About SDG 6

SDG 6 focuses on making sure everyone has access to clean water and safe sanitation. It aims to improve water quality, reduce pollution, and protect freshwater ecosystems.

Main Causes of Water Problems

  • Water pollution from waste and chemicals
  • Lack of clean water facilities
  • Climate change and droughts
  • Poor water management and infrastructure

Solutions to Improve Water Access

  1. Build better water treatment systems
  2. Reduce waste and pollution in rivers and oceans
  3. Educate people about water conservation
  4. Support communities in need of clean water

Did You Know?

Around 2 billion people worldwide live without safe drinking water. SDG 6 aims to provide clean, affordable, and accessible water for everyone by 2030.
